Animals UA Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Animals UA collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects information when preparing and publishing short-form animal, nature, rescue, and travel videos.

1. Who Operates Animals UA

Animals UA is operated as a creator publishing project. It connects to authorized creator accounts only when the account owner approves access through the relevant platform authorization screen.

2. Information Animals UA Collects or Accesses

Animals UA may access information that the project owner explicitly authorizes through connected platforms. This can include selected media files, prepared video files, publishing metadata, and account authorization tokens for services such as YouTube, TikTok, Google Photos, OpenAI, or captioning providers.

Depending on the connected service, Animals UA may process:

  • basic account information needed to confirm the authorized account;
  • videos, thumbnails, captions, titles, descriptions, hashtags, and tags;
  • upload status, publish identifiers, and local processing records;
  • OAuth access and refresh tokens granted by the account owner;
  • technical logs needed to diagnose upload or authorization errors.

3. How Information Is Used

Animals UA uses information only for the public creator workflow:

  • downloading selected videos chosen by the project owner;
  • creating and reviewing titles, descriptions, hashtags, and tags;
  • preparing videos for short-form social platforms;
  • uploading authorized videos to connected creator accounts;
  • tracking local processing status to avoid duplicate uploads.

4. Legal Basis and Account Authorization

Animals UA processes platform account data only after the authorized account owner grants access. The primary basis for processing is the account owner's authorization and the need to perform the requested publishing workflow. Access can be revoked through the connected platform at any time.

5. Information Animals UA Does Not Sell

Animals UA does not sell personal information, account data, videos, tokens, metadata, analytics, or publishing records to third parties.

6. Third-Party Services and Sharing

Animals UA may connect to third-party services, including TikTok, YouTube, Google Photos, OpenAI, and video captioning APIs. Those services process data according to their own privacy policies and account settings.

Animals UA shares information with a third-party service only when that sharing is necessary to complete the requested workflow, such as uploading a video to a connected account or generating captions for a selected video. Animals UA does not provide account data to unrelated advertisers, data brokers, or analytics resellers.

7. Local Storage and Security

Animals UA may store configuration files, OAuth tokens, downloaded videos, generated metadata, and processing state locally on the project owner's computer. The project owner is responsible for securing that device and revoking access through platform account settings when needed.

Animals UA uses access tokens only for authorized API requests and does not publish those tokens. Reasonable technical safeguards are used for local files, but no system can guarantee absolute security.

8. Data Retention

Local files remain available until manually deleted. Published videos and platform data remain available according to the policies and settings of the destination platform.

OAuth tokens and local processing records may be kept while the account remains connected so that Animals UA can continue publishing authorized videos. They can be deleted locally or revoked through the platform account settings.

9. Account Access, Revocation, and Deletion Requests

The project owner can revoke connected account access at any time through the relevant platform settings, including TikTok, Google, or YouTube account security pages.

Requests to remove local records, disconnect a platform account, or stop processing a particular video can be sent through the official social media profile associated with Animals UA. Platform-hosted content may also need to be removed through the destination platform's own tools.

10. User Rights

Depending on local law, users may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, or objection regarding personal information processed by Animals UA. Animals UA will respond to reasonable requests through the official contact channel when the request can be verified.

11. International Processing

Connected platforms and service providers may process information in countries different from the account owner's country. Those transfers are governed by the policies and safeguards of the relevant provider.

12. Children

Animals UA is not intended for use by children and is not designed to collect information from children.

13. Changes

Animals UA may update this Privacy Policy when the publishing workflow or connected services change. The updated version will be posted on this page.

14. Contact

For privacy questions, contact the project owner through the official social media profile associated with Animals UA.
