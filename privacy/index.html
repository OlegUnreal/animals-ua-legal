Animals UA Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Animals UA handles information when preparing and publishing short-form animal, nature, and travel videos.

1. Information Animals UA Accesses

Animals UA may access information that the project owner explicitly authorizes through connected platforms. This can include selected media files, prepared video files, publishing metadata, and account authorization tokens for services such as YouTube, TikTok, Google Photos, OpenAI, or captioning providers.

2. How Information Is Used

Animals UA uses information only for the public creator workflow:

  • downloading selected videos chosen by the project owner;
  • creating and reviewing titles, descriptions, hashtags, and tags;
  • preparing videos for short-form social platforms;
  • uploading authorized videos to connected creator accounts;
  • tracking local processing status to avoid duplicate uploads.

3. Information Animals UA Does Not Sell

Animals UA does not sell personal information, account data, videos, tokens, metadata, analytics, or publishing records to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

Animals UA may connect to third-party services, including TikTok, YouTube, Google Photos, OpenAI, and video captioning APIs. Those services process data according to their own privacy policies and account settings.

5. Local Storage and Security

Animals UA may store configuration files, OAuth tokens, downloaded videos, generated metadata, and processing state locally on the project owner's computer. The project owner is responsible for securing that device and revoking access through platform account settings when needed.

6. Data Retention

Local files remain available until manually deleted. Published videos and platform data remain available according to the policies and settings of the destination platform.

7. Account Access and Revocation

The project owner can revoke connected account access at any time through the relevant platform settings, including TikTok, Google, or YouTube account security pages.

8. Children

Animals UA is not intended for use by children and is not designed to collect information from children.

9. Changes

Animals UA may update this Privacy Policy when the publishing workflow or connected services change. The updated version will be posted on this page.

10. Contact

For privacy questions, contact the project owner through the official social media profile associated with Animals UA.
