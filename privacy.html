Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Animals UA handles information when using app integrations for preparing and publishing short-form animal videos.

1. Information We Access

The app may access information that the project owner explicitly authorizes through third-party platforms. This may include selected media files from Google Photos, video files prepared for upload, and account authorization tokens for platforms such as YouTube or TikTok.

2. How Information Is Used

Information is used only to support the creator workflow:

  • downloading selected videos chosen by the project owner;
  • generating titles, descriptions, hashtags, and tags;
  • preparing videos for short-form platforms;
  • uploading authorized videos to connected accounts.

3. Information We Do Not Sell

Animals UA does not sell personal information, account data, videos, tokens, or metadata to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

The app may connect to third-party services, including Google Photos, YouTube, TikTok, OpenAI, and video captioning or processing APIs. Those services process data according to their own privacy policies and terms.

5. Local Storage

The app may store configuration files, OAuth tokens, downloaded videos, generated metadata, and processing state locally on the project owner's computer. The project owner is responsible for keeping that computer and those files secure.

6. Data Retention

Local files may remain on the project owner's computer until they are manually deleted. Third-party platform data may remain available according to the policies and settings of each platform.

7. Account Access

The project owner can revoke access to connected accounts at any time through the account settings of the relevant platform, such as Google, YouTube, or TikTok.

8. Children

The app is not intended for use by children and is not designed to collect information from children.

9. Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page.

10. Contact

For privacy questions, contact the project owner through the social media profile associated with Animals UA.
